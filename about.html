About ionet

We design, automate, and operate Australia's networks. That's all we do.

ionet is a specialist Australian consultancy — engineers, architects, and NOC operators with careers spent inside ISPs, enterprise networks, smart-building integrators, and managed-service providers. Multi-vendor, code-first, and on-call when it matters. Project-based or long-term managed operations. Same engineers either way.

Why ionet?

Decades of combined network engineering, applied to your environment.

ionet is built around a simple thesis: networks should be designed, automated, and operated by engineers who do this every day — not by generalists treating it as a sideline. Our team has spent careers inside ISPs, enterprise networks, smart-building integrators, and managed-service providers. We bring that operational discipline into every engagement.

We stay deliberately vendor-agnostic. Cisco, Juniper, Nokia, Fortinet, Palo Alto, F5, Ruckus, Unifi, HP / Aruba, DZS, Microsoft Azure — we recommend what fits the architecture, not what pays the highest commission.

Our pillars

Nine disciplines, one team.

Each reinforces the others — good design produces simpler operations, automation lets us scale, ZTP and config management keep estates consistent, monitoring closes the loop, and ICN ties it all to the building.

Network Design

Robust, scalable, multi-vendor networks at the core of every engagement. Campus LAN, data-centre fabrics (VXLAN/EVPN), WAN, MPLS, segment routing. Documented, audited, and maintainable.

Network Automation

Ansible, Python (NAPALM/Netmiko), Terraform, NetBox, CI/CD pipelines for network change. Drift detection, golden configs, and validation before every push.

Zero Touch Provisioning

Production ZTP across Cisco IOS-XE, IOS, Nexus (NX-OS), Juniper Junos, Nokia 7210 and more. DHCP options 66 / 67 / 150, image & config served at first boot. Same template, every site, no engineer at the rack.

Configuration Management

Ansible-driven golden configs across multi-site, multi-vendor estates. Drift detection & remediation, peer-reviewed changes, snapshot + rollback. Consistency on every device, every time.

Monitoring & Observability

Streaming telemetry into Prometheus, InfluxDB, Grafana. SNMP, gNMI, NETCONF, syslog, NetFlow / sFlow. Alerts that humans want to read; dashboards built for engineers, not screensavers.

24/7 Operations

NOC services with on-call rotation across Australian timezones. Fault notification (email, SMS, push), MFA / 2FA-gated portals, SLA-backed responses, quarterly health reports.

Wireless & Wi-Fi

Cisco, Ruckus, Aruba, Unifi WAPs. Predictive & on-site RF surveys, RADIUS / 802.1X, captive portals, mesh, point-to-point, high-density venue design. Wi-Fi 6 / 6E / 7 ready.

Fibre Access — GPON / XGS-PON

Design, deploy, and operate fibre access on DZS (formerly Dasan) and Nokia OLTs. GPON, XGS-PON, XGPON for residential ISPs, enterprise campuses, and smart-building ICN.

ICN & Smart Buildings

Voice, video, data, BMS, access control, AV, IoT — all on one engineered backbone. GPON / XGS-PON access, SIP trunking, SD-WAN, and the operations to back them up.

What makes us different

Network engineering is our core focus — not a side menu.

The networks businesses depend on have got more complex every year — fibre access scaling to XGS-PON, ICN bringing voice, video, BMS, and IoT onto the same backbone, cloud workloads needing dedicated peering, and automation moving from "nice-to-have" to "the only way to scale."

Generalist IT support isn't enough for any of that. You need a team that lives and breathes networks — design, automation, operations — and treats each one as a first-class discipline.

ionet's engineers have run carrier-grade BGP / MPLS networks, designed greenfield campus and data-centre fabrics, automated multi-thousand-device estates with Ansible and Python, and operated 24/7 NOCs with strict SLAs.

That's the operational discipline we bring to your network — whether you need a single sprint of design work or a permanent ops partner.

How we work

Two ways to engage.

Same engineers, same standards, same depth. Different commitment shapes.

Project-based consulting

Discrete scope, defined deliverables, agreed timeline. Network design, automation rollouts, fibre / wireless builds, Azure migrations, brownfield refreshes, NOC stand-up. Hand-off to your team or to ours.

Managed network operations

We run the network long-term — 24/7 NOC, fault notification (email, SMS, push), MFA-gated change portals, scheduled patching, capacity reviews, quarterly health reports. SLA-backed, renewable annually, no fixed end date.

Embedded engineers

Senior engineers slotted into your sprints. Network engineers, automation specialists, NOC operators. Time-and-materials or fixed monthly retainers. We use your tooling and ticketing.
